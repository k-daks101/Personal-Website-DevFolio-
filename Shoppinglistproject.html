Project Details

The Shopping List project is a user-friendly website designed for managing shopping lists. Users can effortlessly add, remove, or update items, ensuring organized and efficient shopping experiences. The list is persistent, retaining data even after the page is closed. Perfect for anyone looking for a straightforward and practical way to plan their shopping trips online!
